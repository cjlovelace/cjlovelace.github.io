Introduction to Our Research

This SURE Research project conducted by Cody Lovelace under the guidance of Dr. Bruce Maxim at the University of Michigan-Dearborn aims to explore the user experience of disabled users of virtual reality. As a new chapter of the digital frontier, academic research surrounding virtual reality is currently limited in scope and depth.

By analyzing current scholarly articles in this field, categorizing into common themes, and using this knowledge to implement accessibility tools into an existing Unity project, we hope to contribute academic research that helps shape the understanding and future progress to improve the experiences of all users of virtual reality.
